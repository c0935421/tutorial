Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions


Q: What types of ceiling fans do you offer?

A: We offer a wide range of ceiling fans including traditional, modern, industrial, and smart fans. Our selection caters to different styles and needs.

Q: How do I choose the right ceiling fan for my room?

A: Choosing the right ceiling fan depends on the room size, ceiling height, and your design preferences. Our experts can help you find the perfect fan based on these factors.

Q: Do your ceiling fans come with a warranty?

A: Yes, all our ceiling fans come with a comprehensive warranty. The length and terms of the warranty vary by product, so please check the product details or contact us for more information.

Installation & Maintenance


Q: Do you provide installation services?

A: Yes, we offer professional installation services for all the ceiling fans we sell. Our team of experts ensures safe and efficient installation.

Q: How do I maintain my ceiling fan?

A: Regular maintenance includes dusting the fan blades and checking for any loose screws. For detailed maintenance instructions, refer to the user manual or contact our support team.

Q: Can I install a ceiling fan on a sloped ceiling?

A: Yes, we have ceiling fan models designed for sloped ceilings, and we offer mounting kits that can adapt most of our fans to sloped installations.

Orders & Shipping


Q: How do I track my order?

A: After your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number on our website to track the delivery status of your order.

Q: What are your shipping options?

A: We offer various shipping options including standard, expedited, and overnight shipping. Shipping costs and times depend on your location and selected shipping method.

Q: Can I return or exchange a ceiling fan?

A: Yes, we have a flexible return and exchange policy. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, please contact our customer service within 30 days for assistance.
